Who I Am

I'm Quinn Koch, a fourth-year undergraduate at UCLA pursuing a B.A. in Geography and a B.S. in Statistics and Data Science, graduating in June 2026. My work sits at the intersection of earth system science and quantitative methods — I'm interested in understanding how climate variability, vegetation, and disturbance regimes like wildfire are linked across timescales.

I'm heading toward a PhD in the earth or environmental sciences, where I hope to continue using remote sensing, paleoecological records, and computational models to reconstruct past fire-climate dynamics and better anticipate future ones.
