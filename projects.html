Research & Projects

Senior Thesis · Ongoing

Pre-Industrial Wildfire Regimes in the Western US

Reconstructing historical fire return intervals in the western United States by integrating paleoecological records and expert fire data with the coupled climate-forest ecosystem-fire models DYNAFFOREST and WULFFSS, run on NCAR supercomputing infrastructure. Advised by Dr. Park Williams, UCLA Geography.

NASA SARP · Summer 2025

Post-Fire Ecosystem Recovery via Spectral Indices & Flux Towers

Used Landsat multispectral data and eddy-covariance flux tower records to quantify carbon flux recovery trajectories in post-fire forest ecosystems across different burn severities and forest types. Presented at AGU 2025.
